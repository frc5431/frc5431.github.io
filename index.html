TITAN ROBOTICS strives to develop student leaders by collaborating to solve complex problems.

We are 5431 robotics, and we participate in the FRC robotics competition. We use science, technology, math, and engineering to build and program our robots. Titan Robotics strives to develop student leaders through working together to create solutions to complex problems. We inspire and motivate each other to become thoughtful, disciplined, and creative thinkers in a professional atmosphere.

What is FRC?

FIRST stands for For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology. It was fundamentally created to inspire students of all levels to understand, use, and enjoy science and technology. Today, 90% of FIRST alumni are pursuing a career in STEM fields.
