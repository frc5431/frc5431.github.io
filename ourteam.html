Our Team

Titan Robotics is a FIRST Robotics Competition team located in Plano, Texas.

Titan Robotics is a FIRST Robotics Competition Team. Each year since 2015 we have strived to promote the values of FIRST, support our community, and build robots. Our team is composed of sub-teams specializing in different fields.

Our Subteams

Build

The Build subteam is mainly focused on designing mechanical components of the robot and physical projects needed for the team. Some specializations in the build subteam include CADing, welding, mill and lathe, and general assembly.

Programming

The Programming subteam is composed of two groups: the programmers and the electricians. Programming members work on projects that focus on automating mechanical components. Programming’s responsibilities consist of wiring, arranging pneumatics, coding, and designing robot control systems.

Scouting/Strategy

The Scouting and Strategy subteam is a crucial part of the team, especially during competitions. This subteam learns about other robots, studies gameplay, knows the game rules, and teaches the team to gather data on the teams competing.

Marketing

Even robots need to look pretty! The Marketing subteam is directed toward digital design and social media presence. This group works on designing event fliers and t-shirts, crafting decorations, posting on our social media, editing videos, and building websites.

SOFA

This subteam works on Sponsorships, Outreach, Fundraising, and Awards. This team displays our robot at local events, runs summer camps, promotes STEAM education, competes in Chairman’s Presentations, Woodie Flowers awards, and shares the team’s story with our community.

PIMO

This is our Pit Inventory, Management, and Organization subteam. They are in charge of ordering parts for our robot, organizing and managing where the parts are and how many we have, and keeping the closet and pit clean and organized.
