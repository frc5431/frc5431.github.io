Our Values

At the heart of how we conduct ourselves as a team are our values.

Respect

When communicating and participating in this student-led team all members must be aware of othersâ€™ feelings and traditions. While working together our students value the input of mentors who take time to teach and help around. Our mentors treat the students as individuals with the right and responsibility to make decisions. 5431 is a team that relies on respect and the courtesy of our members to build a successful robot.

Honesty

In our team it is important that our communication is as sincere as possible. When a new idea comes into play it is important to know how everyone feels about it. Having sincere communication also means we are more open to helping each other; from different sub-teams within the team or in interpersonal communication between FRC teams. With this honesty, we can build a close community which brings us to our next value of Teamwork!

Openness

We value openness among our subteams and teammates to keep the entire team in the loop. Before a robotics meeting concludes, we set aside time for updates. During this time, each subteam lead will give a summary about what they accomplished during the meeting. This allows the team to work fluidly together and contribute to the thought process. A subteam could help another subteam come up with a unique solution so that is why we value openness.

Teamwork

Comprised of various sub teams, Titan Robotics understands the need for teamwork. Each subteam fills an important role through expertise, with in various projects members of each subteam work together to create videos, speeches, code, and mechanical components. By working with other teams we are able to host an NTX Robotics Off-Season Tournament each year, bringing fun experiences to robotics teams and enabling us to share the benefits that come with hosting an event.

Accountability

Our team believes in taking responsibility for our words, actions, assignments, and projects. Accountability enables us to use our time and resources productively. By using project timeline softwares we are able to assign jobs, due dates, and resources to specific projects. Doing this allows us to hold ourselves accountable and achieve great things!

Learning

We take learning seriously while having fun with the team. During the fall our team hosts a Coach and Mentor Workshop where different teams and coaches come together to offer tips and advice to each other. When our team is being mentored by a professional, we watch and take notes so we can learn more about what they do.

Commitment

Robotics requires us to make a commitment to each other and to our school. Every single team member is dedicated to making each project, each robot, and each year the best it can be. When everyone is committed to the team, we can rely on each other.
